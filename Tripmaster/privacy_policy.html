01

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for the personal data of Classic Rally Tripmaster users is:

  • Developer:  Menkaura Soft
  • Application:  Classic Rally Tripmaster (development name: HaldaTripMaster)
  • Contact:  menkaurasoft@gmail.com

By downloading and using Classic Rally Tripmaster, the user accepts the terms described in this policy, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 on Personal Data Protection (LOPDGDD).

02

Data We Collect

Classic Rally Tripmaster is an offline tool designed for rally navigation. The app collects only the data strictly necessary for its operation:

  • User settings:  Configuration parameters such as units, distance calibration and display preferences, stored locally on the device.
  • Route and stage data:  Distances, waypoints and timing records entered by the user, stored locally on the device.
  • Anonymous usage data:  Aggregated and anonymised information about feature usage to improve the app, only if the user has consented to analytics.
Important: Classic Rally Tripmaster does not collect your name, email address, phone number, GPS location or any other personally identifiable information, unless you voluntarily provide it via the contact form.
03

Purpose of Processing

The data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Provide tripmaster and odometer functions for rally navigation.
  • Save the user's preferences and configuration to personalise the in-app experience.
  • Improve the quality of the service through anonymous usage statistics (only with the user's consent).
  • Respond to queries or support requests voluntarily submitted by the user.
Our commitment: We do not use your data for targeted advertising, commercial profiling or any purpose other than those described in this policy.
04

Local Storage

Classic Rally Tripmaster is designed to work primarily without an internet connection. All data handling follows these principles:

  • All routes, settings and stage records are stored exclusively on the user's device.
  • Locally stored data is not synchronised with external servers and is not shared with third parties without the user's explicit consent.
  • Data is stored in the app's private storage space, inaccessible to other applications on the device.
  • Uninstalling the application automatically and permanently deletes all locally stored data.
Your routes and configuration never leave your device unless you choose to export them manually or share a diagnostic report with support.
05

Third-Party Services

Classic Rally Tripmaster may integrate the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

Google Play Services Required for the app to function on Android devices. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com.
Firebase (Google) Optionally used for anonymous usage analytics and update management. Any transmitted data is anonymous and aggregated.

We do not sell, transfer or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes under any circumstances.

06

Data Retention

Configuration and route data is stored exclusively on the user's device and deleted automatically when the application is uninstalled.

Any anonymous and aggregated usage data that may be transmitted to our servers is retained for a maximum of 24 months, after which it is permanently deleted.

If you contact us by email, the data you provide is retained only for as long as needed to handle your request, and for a maximum of 12 months.

07

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

AccessKnow what data we hold about you.
RectificationCorrect inaccurate or incomplete data.
ErasureRequest deletion ("right to be forgotten").
RestrictionLimit how your data is processed.

ObjectionObject to processing in certain cases.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at menkaurasoft@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD) at www.aepd.es.

08

Security

We apply technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, loss, alteration or improper disclosure:

  • Encrypted communications via HTTPS/TLS for all server connections.
  • Data stored in the app's private storage, not accessible to other applications.
  • Periodic review of implemented security measures.
  • Server-side data access restricted to authorised Menkaura Soft personnel only.
09

Minors

Classic Rally Tripmaster is not directed at children under the age of 14. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe your child has provided data through the app, please contact us at menkaurasoft@gmail.com and we will delete it as soon as possible.
